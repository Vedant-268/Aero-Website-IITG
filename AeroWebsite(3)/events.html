OUR  LATEST

EVENTS

OPEN-CV WORKSHOP


With hands-on exercises and practical examples, participants learn how to transform images and video into powerful tools that can detect and recognize objects, track motion, and more

SKYSPRINT


Skyprint is a nation wide competition to be conducted by the Aero Club in collaboration with Techniche during 31st Aug - 3rd Sep at IIT Guwahati. The problem statement is to design, manufacture, and demonstrate a fixed-wing aircraft system by assembling a capable team that can meet the requirements outlined in the following rounds.

WINGWIZARDS


WingWizards was an engaging and interactive Fusion 360 workshop that aimed to introduce participants to the world of 3D modeling and design. Led by experienced instructors, this workshop provided attendees with a hands-on learning experience in using Fusion 360 software, a powerful tool for creating digital models and prototypes.

DRONE-BUILDING
WORKSHOP


The Drone Building workshop is an immersive and practical experience where participants learn how to construct their very own drone from scratch. During the workshop, participants work in small groups, guided through each step of the assembly process. From attaching the frame to installing motors, propellers, and the flight controller.






COMPETITIONS

WE HAVE   PARTICIPATED IN


INTER-IIT


The Aero Club, IIT Guwahati secured two consecutive silver medals in Inter IIT 2019 & 2020.We were placed at 4th position in inter IIT 10.0 in DRDO’s UAV- GUIDED UGV NAVIGATION CHALLENGE. This year, we participated in the High Prep Problem statement floated by DRONA AVIATION and IIT Kanpur. The club also presented one of its projects - Reaper in one of its Low Prep problem statements “E Conclave”.

SAE


The SAE Aeromodelling Competition is an annual event organized by the Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) that challenges teams of students to design, build, and fly radio-controlled aircraft. Aero club, IIT Guwahati is going to participate in SAE 2023 to be conducted on 21st to 23rd July in Chennai.

TECHEVINCE


The Aero club was one of the many clubs that participated in Techevince 9.0. The club showcased its latest projects related to the field of aeronautics and aviation, and received positive feedback from the attendees and judges.Certainly, one of the highlights of Techevince 9.0 was the audience choice award, which was presented to Project Raven.

BOEING AERMODELLING   CHALLENGE


Boeing India Aeromodelling Competition is a national level competition and organised by Boeing India. Our team participated in the IIT Kharagpur zonal event more popularly known as Laws of Motion held from 21st January to 23rd January 2023. Aeromodelling Club IITG finished in Top 20 zonal finalists held in IIT Kharagpur.
